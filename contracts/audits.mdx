The V5.1 internal audit catalogued 5 critical, 14 high-severity, and a long
tail of medium / low findings against the protocol. Verdict: **NEEDS-FIXES**
at commit `6a3ce42`.

Most of the high-severity findings have been fixed in dedicated branches. The
mainnet rollout is gated on completion of the remaining items.

|---|---|---|---|

| High | 14 | 6 | 8 |
| Medium | ~20 | 12 | rest |
| Low / preventive | ~5 | 3 | 2 |

A live status table is maintained at
[`org-lumina/LUMINA-PROTOCOL/AUDIT_STATUS.md`](https://github.com/org-lumina/LUMINA-PROTOCOL/blob/main/AUDIT_STATUS.md)
in the protocol repo (when present).

## Independent audit

An independent audit by a third-party firm is planned before mainnet. The
internal audit is what gates the *internal* sign-off for testnet promotion.

## Reporting a vulnerability

If you find something we haven't, please don't open a public issue. Email
**security@lumina-org.com** with reproduction steps. Bounties are scoped to
mainnet contracts (Sepolia mocks excluded).
